# Cultural Protocol

## Guiding Principle

Knowledge is not only data.

It is carried by people.
It is rooted in place.
It is shaped by language.
It is protected by protocol.
It is renewed through practice.
It is inherited with responsibility.

Culturalverse exists to help learners approach that responsibility with respect.

## Protocol Commitments

- Honor source communities and cultural stewards.
- Keep context, attribution, and place connected to knowledge.
- Avoid flattening sacred or living traditions into generic assets.
- Use language, names, and symbols with care.
- Design interactions that guide humility, not extraction.
