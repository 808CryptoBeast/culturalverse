Guiding Principle

Knowledge is not only data. It is carried by people. It is rooted in place. It is shaped by language. It is protected by protocol. It is renewed through practice. It is inherited with responsibility.

Cultural Protocol

Approach each tradition with humility, attribution, and context. Do not extract sacred material from living communities without invitation.

  • Respect origin communities and stewards.
  • Prioritize context over novelty.
  • Document sources and attribution clearly.
