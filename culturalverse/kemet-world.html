Protocol Anchors

Before You Enter

Kemet is ancient and living — its contemporary practitioners and scholars are the bridge between archaeological record and living tradition.

  • Distinguish living heritage from pop symbolism — engage the historical and scholarly record before making claims about meaning.
  • Trace claims to grounded historical context — Kemet belongs to its African geographic and cultural continuum, not to abstracted universal mysticism.
  • Honor continuity between ritual, language, and place — contemporary African and diaspora communities carry living threads of this tradition.
